## #1: MOBILE CAR DETAILING

### Why It Wins
- Lowest startup cost
- Fastest time to profit
- Everyone needs car detailing (high demand)
- Can start part-time
- Mobile = no shop needed

### Real Numbers
- 3 cars/day × $150 avg = $450/day
- 20 working days = $9,000/month revenue
- Expenses: supplies $1,000, insurance $500, gas $300
- **NET PROFIT: ~$7,200/month**

### Startup Steps
1. Buy supplies: shop vac, towels, polisher, chemicals ($2,000)
2. Register LLC ($800)
3. Get insurance ($500/year)
4. Create Google Business Profile
5. Post on Nextdoor, Facebook

### Differentiation Ideas
- Subscription plans (detail monthly)
- Fleet accounts (local businesses)
- Same-day service
- Before/after photo portfolio

## #2: PRESSURE WASHING

### Why It Works
- Seasonal demand (spring/fall)
- Residential + commercial markets
- Can bundle with other services

### Real Numbers
- 4 jobs/week × $250 = $1,000/week
- $4,000/month revenue
- Expenses: $500
- **NET PROFIT: ~$3,500/month**

### Startup Steps
1. Buy pressure washer ($2,500-4,000)
2. Get surface cleaner, chemicals ($400)
3. Register LLC, get insurance
4. Marketing: flyers, Nextdoor

## #3: CERAMIC COATING

### The Opportunity
- High ticket = big money
- Premium pricing
- Mobile = no shop
- Trucks/SUVs in Texas = huge market

### The Catch
- Need skill/training
- Practice on beaters first
- Build portfolio before charging premium

### Real Numbers
- 3 cars/week × $700 = $2,100/week
- $8,400/month revenue
- **NET PROFIT: ~$6,500/month**

---

## #4: TRUCK RENTAL

### The Good
- Niche focus (work trucks only)
- Less competition than car rental
- Body shop partnerships = steady work

### The Bad
- $90K+ startup needed
- Slow to profit
- Insurance is expensive

### Better Approach
- Start with 1 truck, self-finance
- Partner with body shops first

## #5: EVENT RENTAL

- Repeat business potential

### The Issues
- Need storage space

- Seasonal (spring/fall busy)

## Recommended MVP Path

### Phase 1: Car Detailing (Month 1-2)
- Spend $500 on supplies
- Detail 5 cars for free (build portfolio)
- Post before/after pics
- Start charging $150/car

### Phase 2: Scale (Month 3-6)
- Increase to 3 cars/day
- Add pressure washing as upsell
- Reinvest in better equipment

### Phase 3: Level Up (Month 6+)
- Add ceramic coating
- Hire help
- Build fleet accounts
