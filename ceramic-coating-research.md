## SKILL REQUIREMENTS

### Paint Correction Process
1. Wash
2. Decontaminate (clay)
3. Assess paint (paint thickness meter)
4. Compound (if needed)
5. Polish
6. Wipe down (IPA)
7. Apply coating
8. Cure/harden
9. Buff off

### Time Estimates
- Paint correction only: 4-8 hours
- Full detail + coating: 8-12 hours
- Express coating: 3-4 hours
