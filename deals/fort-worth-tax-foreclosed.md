## Key Insights

### Why These Work:
1. **Extremely low purchase price** - Under $10K
2. **City-owned** - Clear title process
3. **Fort Worth growing** - Good appreciation potential
4. **Multiple units available** - Can buy portfolio

### Risks:
1. **Unknown property condition** - May need significant rehab

3. **As-is** - No warranties
4. **May have liens** - Verify title

1. Contact City of Fort Worth: (817) 392-6253
2. Request specific property details
