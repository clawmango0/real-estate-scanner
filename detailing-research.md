## RISK ANALYSIS

### Startup Risks
- [ ] Equipment breaks - have backup plan
- [ ] Customer no-shows - require deposits
- [ ] Weather days - indoor spaces? reschedule policy?
- [ ] Injuries - insurance covers?
- [ ] Damage to car - liability insurance?
- [ ] Low demand - marketing budget ready?

### Mitigation Strategies
| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| Equipment failure | Medium | High | Buy quality, have backup |
| Low customers | High | High | Pre-marketing before launch |
| Weather | Medium | Medium | Flexible scheduling |
| Damage claim | Low | High | Insurance, documentation |
