7 Investment Strategies

Buy & Hold

CoC return, monthly cash flow, 5-year Schedule E tax worksheet, 5/10/15-year exit projections with depreciation recapture and LTCG tax.

Fix & Flip

ARV from condition/improvement matrix, 70% MAO rule, holding costs, hard money modeling, net profit and ROI.

BRRRR

Buy-rehab-rent-refi-repeat: ARV calculation, refi at 75% LTV after seasoning, capital recycled, infinite return detection.

Short-Term Rental

Commercial / Multi

Cap rate, DSCR, GRM, price-per-unit, NOI with vacancy adjustment for duplexes through apartment complexes.

Passive / Syndication

IRR via Newton's method, equity multiple, preferred return, annual distributions for limited partner investments.

Powered by Data

Every analysis is built on multiple independent data sources — no single source tells the whole story.

🏠

Zillow + Redfin + Realtor.com

Real-time property listings with price, beds, baths, sqft, lot size, and Rent Zestimates from email alerts and bookmarklet capture.

📊

Zillow ZHVI & ZORI

Home Value Index and Observed Rent Index at the ZIP code level. Updated monthly to calibrate rent estimates against actual market data.

🏛️

U.S. Census Bureau

Median household income, population, and housing units per ZIP from the American Community Survey. Powers affordability ratios.

🎓

NCES & Texas Education Agency

School quality ratings (1-10) per ZIP code. A-rated districts command 6-12% rent premiums in our estimation model.

🛡️

FBI UCR & Bureau of Justice

Crime safety scores (1-10) per ZIP. High-crime areas face higher vacancy and lower rents — factored into every projection.

📍

Census Bureau Geocoder

USPS-verified addresses with city, state, ZIP, and lat/lng. Every property is geocoded — no null locations.

🤖

Claude AI (Anthropic)

AI-powered email parsing, listing extraction, and rent estimation. Handles every email format from Zillow digests to Redfin marketing.

📋

IRS 2025 Tax Tables

Full Schedule E with all 16 expense categories, cost segregation, bonus depreciation, Section 179, and passive activity loss rules.

Built for Texas Investors

DFW-Calibrated Rent Model

9-factor rent estimation engine calibrated to the Dallas-Fort Worth market: yield-by-price-tier, ZHVI comparison, property type, bed/bath adjustment, sqft floor/cap, condition, improvement, market heat, and school district premium.

Market Analytics

Per-project analytics: CoC distribution, price-to-rent ratios by city, neighborhood performance, cash flow vs. appreciation scatter plot, budget position, and portfolio 5-year projection.

Deal Pipeline

Properties auto-stage from Inbox through Shortlist, Due Diligence, Offer, Contract, to Closed. Risk flags warn about missing data, negative cash flow, and stale listings.

Privacy First

All financial calculations run in your browser. Your data is stored securely in Supabase with row-level security. No data is shared with third parties.
