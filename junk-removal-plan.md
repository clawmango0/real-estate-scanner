# Junk Removal Business Plan - Fort Worth
*Created: February 27, 2026*

---

## Why Junk Removal?

### Pros
- **Low startup** - $1,500-3,000 to start
- **High demand** - Everyone has junk, few want to deal with it
- **Simple business** - No special licenses, no training needed
- **Recurring potential** - Estate cleanouts, hoarder situations
- **Physical but not exhausting** - Loading trucks vs pressure washing

### Cons
- **Labor intensive** - You're hauling stuff
- **Need disposal fees** - Dump rental/landfill costs
- **Truck required** - Need a pickup or trailer

## Pricing Model

### Load-Based Pricing
| Size | Price | Typical Job |
|------|-------|--------------|
| Small load | $75-125 | Minor decluttering |
| 1/4 truck | $125-175 | Garage cleanout |
| 1/2 truck | $175-250 | Room/hallway |
| Full truck | $300-400 | Whole house |

### Hourly Pricing (Alternative)
- $50-75/hour for 2-person crew
- $75-100/hour for 3-person crew

### Add-ons
- **Same-day service**: +$25-50
- **After hours**: +$25-50
- **Heavy items** (pianos, safes): +$50-100 each
- **Basement/attic stairs**: +$25-50
- **Estate cleanouts**: Discount 10-15% for big jobs

## Competition in Fort Worth

### Major Players
- **1-800-GOT-JUNK?** - Big franchise, expensive
- **College Hunks Hauling Junk** - Franchise, mid-range
- **Junk King** - Franchise

### Opportunity
- **Franchises are expensive** - $5K-10K+ to join
- **Inconsistent pricing** - Often $200+ for small jobs
- **Slow scheduling** - Often 2-3 day wait
- **Local independents** - Few and far between

### Differentiation
- **Same-day service** - Most can't match
- **Cheaper than franchises** - undercut by 20-30%
- **Estate/hoarder special** - Niche market
- **Real estate agents** - Partner for vacant cleanouts
- **Property managers** - Recurring landlord business

## How to Get Customers

### 1. Google Business Profile
- Free, ranks locally
- Get 5-star reviews
- Photos of your truck/team

### 2. Nextdoor
- Perfect for homeowners
- Post "Your neighborhood junk removal"
- $0 cost

### 3. Facebook Marketplace
- Post in "For Sale" + "Free"
- People giving away items = your leads

### 4. Real Estate Agents
- Vacant property cleanouts
- Estate sales done
- Foreclosure prep
- **Offer 10% referral fee**

### 5. Property Managers
- Landlord turnover cleanouts
- Eviction cleanouts
- Recurring accounts

### 6. Yelp & Thumbtack
- Pay for leads
- $20-50 per job in fees
