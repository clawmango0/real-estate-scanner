## PART 1: ESSENTIAL TOOLS

### 1.1 Booking & Scheduling

| Tool | Use | Cost | Rating |
|------|-----|------|--------|
| **Jobber** | All-in-one scheduling, invoicing, CRM | $29-99/mo | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| **ServiceTitan** | Enterprise-grade | $99+/mo | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| **Calendly** | Simple scheduling | Free-$12/mo | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| **Square Appointments** | Free with Square | Free | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
